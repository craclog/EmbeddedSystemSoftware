How to Play : 
*************************************************

1. Press "PUZZLE START" Button.

2. Input [row col] to generate Buttons. (row : 1~5, col : 1~5)

ex) 2 2
/* But, [1 1] is NOT allowed. */

3. Black Button means Empty Space, You can move button to empty space 
   by Click button next to the empty space.

4. Elapsed time is at the top right.

5. Align all the numbers and position the empty space to the bottom right to win the game.

6. Complete the PUZZLE!
